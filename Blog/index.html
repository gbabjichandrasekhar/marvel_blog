About Marvel Studios

Marvel Studios is a film and television production company that creates movies, television shows, and brands. It's based at the Walt Disney Studios in Burbank, California. Marvel Studios is one of the world's most successful movie studios.

Marvel Studios was originally called Marvel Films when it was founded in 1993 by Marvel Entertainment Group. In 1996, it relaunched as Marvel Studios. From 1996 to 2015, Marvel Studios was a subsidiary of Marvel Entertainment, which is a subsidiary of The Walt Disney Company. In 2015, Marvel Studios was integrated directly into The Walt Disney Studios.

Marvel Studios is responsible for the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), which includes over 30 movies and more than a dozen series. Marvel Studios' divisions include:

  • Marvel Studios Animation
  • Marvel Music
  • Marvel Television (production label)
